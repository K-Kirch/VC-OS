# Playbook — Syndicating a Deal

**When to use:** You're leading or co-leading a round and need to identify and approach suitable co-investors.

---

## Step 1 — Screen for fit (brief.md)

Read `brief.md` first. Check in this order:

1. **Deployment status** — Is the fund actively deploying? If Raising New Fund or Between Funds, deprioritize unless the relationship is strong.
2. **Fit: Syndication** — Pre-assessed fit rating. If Low, skip unless you have a strong reason.
3. **Portfolio conflicts** — Any of their portfolio companies competing with the deal? If yes, stop. They cannot invest.
4. **Intro preference + best entry point** — How do you reach them?

If all green: proceed to Step 2.

---

## Step 2 — Confirm check size and ownership fit (profile.md)

Read `profile.md`. Verify:

- Does their check size range fit the round you're building? (e.g. if you need €2M from a co-investor, their max check must cover it)
- Does their ownership target conflict with your cap table? (e.g. if they need 15% and you're already giving 20% to the lead, it won't work)
- Does the deal stage match their stage focus?
- Does the sector match?

---

## Step 3 — Understand their syndication behavior (co-investors.md)

Read `co-investors.md`. Determine:

- Do they typically lead, follow, or either? If they only lead and you already have a lead, this won't work.
- Have they co-invested with your fund before? Check `relationships.md` for prior co-investments — prior positive experience accelerates everything.
- Are there any funds already in the deal that they avoid?

---

## Step 4 — Identify the right partner (team.md → brief.md)

Read `team.md` for sector/stage coverage. Cross-reference with `brief.md` partner summary for style and approachability. Pick the single right person to approach — not the most senior, the most relevant.

---

## Step 5 — Plan the outreach (relationships.md)

Read `relationships.md`:

- Is there a warm intro path? Use it. A founder mutual is stronger than a fund mutual.
- If cold: check `intro_preference` in `profile.md` — cold email only if "Cold email OK".
- Note last contact date — if someone at your firm spoke to them recently, loop that person in before reaching out.

---

## What to bring to the conversation

- Deal one-pager (company, round size, current cap table, your check, what you're looking for from co-investor)
- Why you thought of them specifically (reference their thesis, a portfolio company, a partner's public writing)
- Timeline — give them a closing date up front

---

## Common failure modes

- Approaching a fund that has a portfolio conflict (check `portfolio.md` conflict column)
- Approaching a fund between funds (check `deployment_status`)
- Pitching the wrong partner (check `team.md` sector focus)
- Cold outreach to a warm-intro-only fund (check `intro_preference`)
