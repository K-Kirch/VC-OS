# Memo Schema — `memo.md`

The IC memo. One file, structured. Each section has a word budget — exceed it only with explicit justification in `notes.md`.

Detail lives in working files (`research.md`, `competition.md`, `risks.md`, `syndication.md`, `conflicts.md`). `memo.md` synthesizes.

## Sections

| # | Section | Budget | Purpose |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | TL;DR & Recommendation | 150 words | One paragraph: thesis + ask + key terms |
| 2 | Deal Snapshot | Table | Round, valuation, our check, ownership, co-investors, timeline |
| 3 | Company | 100 words | What they do (one sentence), founded, location, team size |
| 4 | Problem & Why Now | 250 words | The wedge and the timing argument |
| 5 | Product | 250 words | What it is, how it works, what's defensible |
| 6 | Market | 300 words | TAM/SAM/SOM, dynamics, adjacent expansion |
| 7 | Business Model & Unit Economics | 250 words | Pricing, CAC, payback, gross margin |
| 8 | Traction | 250 words | Revenue, growth, retention, key logos |
| 9 | Team | 250 words | Founders, key hires, gaps, why this team wins |
| 10 | Competition & Moat | 250 words | Direct, indirect, what compounds — detail in `competition.md` |
| 11 | Go-to-Market | 200 words | Current motion + next 12–24 months |
| 12 | Financials & Plan | 250 words | Historicals + projection, milestones to next round |
| 13 | Risks & Mitigations | 300 words | Top 5 — full register in `risks.md` |
| 14 | Deal Terms & Structure | 200 words | Round mechanics, governance, pro-rata |
| 15 | Exit Thesis & Return Scenarios | 250 words | Base/upside/downside, who acquires |
| 16 | Fit with Our Fund | 150 words | Mandate match — references root `CLAUDE.md` operating fund |
| 17 | Portfolio Conflicts | Reference | One-line summary + link to `conflicts.md` |
| 18 | Syndication Plan | Reference | One-line summary + link to `syndication.md` |
| 19 | Open Questions & Conditions to Close | List | What we still need to know / make true |
| 20 | Recommendation | 100 words | Vote ask, sized, with conditions — formal record in `recommendation.md` |

## Hard rules

- Every claim traces to evidence in `dataroom/` or a working file. No unsupported assertions.
- Sections 17, 18, 20 are summaries with links — never duplicate the canonical file.
- Numbers carry source attribution (founder-provided / our analysis / public).
- Use `Unknown` explicitly; never omit a section.
- Word budgets are hard limits unless explicitly justified.
