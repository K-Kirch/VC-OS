# Playbook 4a — Memo Drafting

**When to use:** Research synthesis complete. Now write the argument.

## Steps

1. **Open `memo.md`** — follow `_config/memo-schema.md` section by section
2. **Respect word budgets** — overruns logged in `notes.md` "Budget overrides"
3. **Synthesize, don't restate** — pull conclusions from working files; link rather than duplicate
4. **§16 (Fit with Our Fund)** — explicit mapping to operating fund mandate (root `CLAUDE.md`)
5. **§§17, 18, 20** — summaries only, link to canonical files (`conflicts.md`, `syndication.md`, `recommendation.md`)
6. **Cite everything** — every number and quote traces to `dataroom/` or a working file
7. **Draft `recommendation.md`** — proposed vote, conditions, sponsoring partner

## Hard rules

- No claim without evidence
- No section omitted — use `Unknown` if truly unknown
- Word budgets enforced

## Exit criterion

`memo.md` complete; `recommendation.md` drafted.
