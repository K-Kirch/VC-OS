# Ampli Ventures — Investment Thesis

## Thesis Summary

100% focus on fast-growing Nordic B2B SaaS at early stages (pre-Series A through Series A). Long-term outlook with no mandated exit timeline. Operator-led approach: partners apply hands-on GTM and strategic support drawn from operating roles at iZettle, Meltwater, Soundtrack Your Brand, and consulting at Bain.

## Stage Rationale

Pre-Series A through Series A — the inflection point where GTM execution typically becomes the binding constraint on growth, where the partners' operator backgrounds add the most value.

## Sector Rationale

B2B SaaS / software exclusively. Both partners have deep B2B SaaS operating experience; thesis is to apply that experience as concentrated value-add rather than diversify across sectors.

## What They Look For

- Fast-growing Nordic B2B SaaS at pre-A / Series A
- Strong go-to-market potential
- Founders open to hands-on GTM and strategic input
- Long-term value-creation orientation
- Software businesses with clear repeatable sales motion

## What They Avoid

- Non-SaaS / non-software business models
- Consumer or non-B2B verticals
- Stages outside pre-A / Series A
