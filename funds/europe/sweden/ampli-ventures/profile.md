## Description

Ampli Ventures is a Stockholm-based VC fund founded in 2022, investing €1–5M tickets in early-stage Nordic B2B SaaS companies. Co-founded by ex-Bain consultant Adrian Iredahl and ex-iZettle / Soundtrack Your Brand commercial leader Hannah Meiton, the firm positions itself as operator-led with a GTM-heavy value-add. Founder-friendly long-term outlook with no mandated exit timeline.
