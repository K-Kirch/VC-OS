## TL;DR

Sweden's largest dedicated VC, structured as an evergreen foundation with ~5B SEK AUM. Founded 1979 by the Swedish government, operating independently. Three sector practices — Deep Tech, Life Science, Transformative Tech — each with a dedicated Practice Lead. Patient, science-led capital; 60+ portfolio companies and 18 IPOs over fund history including Oatly, Arcam (acquired GE), Bonesupport, CellaVision, Nuevolution.

## Fit by Use Case

### Syndicating a deal
**Fit:** High (for deep-tech, life-science, or capital-intensive transformative-tech Nordic deals)
**Rationale:** Evergreen structure + 10–50M SEK first ticket makes them an ideal anchor or co-lead for Swedish Series A in deep tech and life science. Patient horizon and follow-on capacity reduce graduation risk between rounds. Public-mission DNA aligns with mission-driven founders. Lower fit for pure software / consumer SaaS without deep-tech or scientific moat.
**Right contact:** Patrik Sobocki (Deep Tech) / Jonas Jendi (Life Science, also COO) / Per Anell (Transformative Tech) — route by sector

### Lining up the next round
**Fit:** High (for Nordic portfolio companies in their three sectors)
**Rationale:** Evergreen capacity means they can lead or co-lead Series A/B for Nordic companies and continue ride-along into growth. Strong signal for a Swedish portfolio company to have Industrifonden on the cap table — historically credentialing for NASDAQ Stockholm IPO path (CellaVision, Bonesupport, Calliditas, Nuevolution pattern).
**Right contact:** Practice Lead by sector; CEO Peter Wolpert for strategic / cross-practice deals

### Competing against them
**They lead with:** Patient evergreen capital; deep Swedish ecosystem credibility (40+ years, public mission); ability to follow-on heavily without reserve-cycle pressure; Stockholm IPO track record; sector-specialist Practice Leads
**Their weakness:** Slower decision-making typical of foundation governance (verify); Nordic-only mandate limits relevance for founders prioritizing US-first investors; brand less known outside Nordics for international founders
**How to differentiate:** Speed of decision, international network (US/UK Series B+ relationships), brand outside the Nordics, operator support beyond board-level governance
