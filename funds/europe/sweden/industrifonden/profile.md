## Description

Industrifonden is Sweden's largest dedicated venture capital fund, structured as an evergreen foundation with ~5B SEK AUM. Founded in 1979 by the Swedish government and now operating independently, it backs Nordic breakthrough innovations across three practice areas — Deep Tech, Life Science, and Transformative Tech. Its evergreen structure reinvests exit proceeds back into new ventures, allowing patient horizons aligned with deep-science and capital-intensive industrial bets. Portfolio includes Oatly (NASDAQ IPO 2021), Arcam (acquired by GE for 6B SEK), Bonesupport, Calliditas, Nuevolution, and CellaVision. [S — from website scrape 2026-05-21]
