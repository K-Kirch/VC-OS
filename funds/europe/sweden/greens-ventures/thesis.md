# Greens Ventures — Investment Thesis

## Thesis Summary

Back Nordic pre-seed startups built by founders who are alumni of Nordic unicorns. The bet is that operators who scaled the previous generation of Nordic successes (Spotify, Klarna, iZettle, King, Mojang, Skype, Truecaller, etc.) carry pattern recognition and network advantages that compound at the earliest stages. Greens functions as the first institutional check into that founder cohort.

## Stage Rationale

Pre-seed only — captures founders at their earliest formation, where the alumni-network filter is most differentiating and network-led support is most useful.

## Sector Rationale

Generalist with software-services concentration (22 of disclosed investments). Sector openness is consistent with founder-first sourcing: the filter is who, not what.

## What They Look For

- Founder(s) who held senior roles at a Nordic unicorn / scaled tech success
- Pre-seed stage (often first institutional check)
- Nordic geography
- Software / tech businesses (predominant)

## What They Avoid

- Founders without prior Nordic-scale operator credentials
- Stages beyond pre-seed (later rounds out of mandate)
