## Description

Greens Ventures is a Stockholm-based pre-seed VC vehicle established by Spotify alumni and managed by Dan McCormick. The fund invests in Nordic pre-seed startups whose founders are alumni of Nordic unicorns — a deliberate sourcing filter that channels capital and network through the operator class that built the previous generation of Nordic tech successes.
