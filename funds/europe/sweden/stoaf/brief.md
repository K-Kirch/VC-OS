## TL;DR

Stockholm-based business-angel-powered venture finance and competence house, founded mid-2008 by Lennart Ohlsson (4 founding partners). Now ~20 partners and ~50 certified business angels. Operates multiple sub-funds (Stoaf Start I, Stoaf Start II, Sustainable Energy Angels). Three sector verticals — Deep Tech (Magnus Eriksson), Sustainable Energy Tech (Klaus Gottwald), Life Sciences (Claes Post) — anchored by an experienced GP team and SVCA membership. Useful as a co-investor signal for early-stage Swedish deep-tech, energy-tech, and life-sciences deals.

## Fit by Use Case

### Syndicating a deal
**Fit:** Medium — syndicate-only, useful for Swedish deep-tech / energy-tech / life-sciences seed
**Rationale:** Brings ~50 angels' aggregated capital plus domain GPs. Useful complement to a venture lead.
**Right contact:** Vertical GP — Magnus Eriksson (deep tech), Klaus Gottwald (SET), Claes Post (life sciences)

### Lining up the next round
**Fit:** Low — angel/early-stage scope; not a follow-on lead at Series A+
**Rationale:** Off-stage for institutional Series A+ rounds.
**Right contact:** Per Anders Wärn (CEO) for ecosystem warm intros

### Competing against them
**They lead with:** Stockholm angel network depth, GP domain expertise, "competence house" framing (education, certification)
**Their weakness:** Smaller aggregated tickets vs institutional VCs; collective decision dynamics
**How to differentiate:** Speed, single-decision, larger initial check, dedicated partner attention
