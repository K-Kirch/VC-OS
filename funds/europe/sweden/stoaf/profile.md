## Description

STOAF (Stockholm Business Angels) is a business-angel-powered venture finance and competence house founded in 2008 by Lennart Ohlsson and three co-founders. The organisation has grown to ~20 partners and ~50 certified angels operating across three vertical specialisations — Deep Tech, Sustainable Energy Technology, and Life Sciences — and runs multiple investment sub-funds (Stoaf Start I, Stoaf Start II, Sustainable Energy Angels). It is an SVCA member and combines investment with an explicit education-and-certification mandate for business angels.
