## TL;DR

Creandum is a Stockholm/Berlin seed fund that backs exceptional product-obsessed founders before their potential is obvious. Known for Spotify and Depop at inception, and Trade Republic more recently. One of Europe's most respected seed funds — they move at conviction, not consensus.

## Fit by Use Case

### Syndicating a deal
**Fit:** High
**Rationale:** Creandum leads at seed and actively syndicates with other European funds. Strong believer in tight, high-conviction syndicates over broad rounds.
**Right contact:** Johan Brenner (Stockholm-based founders), Simon Schmincke (German-speaking ecosystem)

### Lining up the next round
**Fit:** Medium — they back from seed but check size caps out below Series A lead
**Rationale:** Creandum can provide bridge capital and warm introductions to Series A leads (Index, Accel, Sequoia). Their brand carries significant weight with growth-stage funds.
**Right contact:** Relevant partner based on founder geography

### Competing against them
**They lead with:** Brand reputation, product conviction, founder-first culture, deep European operator network
**Their weakness:** Limited check size at growth stage; Berlin presence newer and smaller than Stockholm
**How to differentiate:** Speed of decision if you can move faster; sector depth if Creandum is generalist here; larger check if the round is above €10M
