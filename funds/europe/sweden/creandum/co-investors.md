## Syndication Style

Creandum builds small, high-conviction syndicates. They do not build large crowded rounds — they prefer to go in with one or two co-investors who add genuine value. At seed, they often go in solo or with one other fund. They actively support founders in bringing in the right Series A lead when the time comes.
