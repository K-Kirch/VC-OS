## Notable Exits & Unicorns

### Spotify
- **Outcome:** NYSE IPO, April 2018
- **Details:** First investment 2008. Creandum backed Spotify when streaming music was an unproven concept in Europe. The company became the world's dominant music streaming platform.

### iZettle
- **Outcome:** Acquired by PayPal for $2.2B, May 2018
- **Details:** Swedish mobile payments and point-of-sale company. Creandum investment from 2011.

### Klarna
- **Outcome:** Active unicorn — IPO pending
- **Details:** Investment from 2016. Swedish BNPL and payments platform, valued at $6.7B as of last round.

### Depop
- **Outcome:** Acquired by Etsy for $1.625B, June 2021
- **Details:** Social fashion resale marketplace. Creandum backed from seed; Maria Raga (now Creandum Venture Partner) was CEO at time of acquisition.

### Trade Republic
- **Outcome:** Active unicorn ($5B+ valuation)
- **Details:** Investment 2019. German retail investing platform.

### Pleo
- **Outcome:** Active unicorn ($4.7B valuation, 2022)
- **Details:** Investment 2016. Danish expense management platform.

### Bolt
- **Outcome:** Active unicorn ($8.4B valuation)
- **Details:** Investment 2019. Estonian ride-hailing and mobility platform.
