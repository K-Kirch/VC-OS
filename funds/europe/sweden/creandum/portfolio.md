## Notable Exits & Unicorns

### Spotify
- **Outcome:** IPO on NYSE, 2018
- **Details:** Creandum invested at seed in 2006 when Spotify had 3 employees. The company grew to become the world's largest music streaming platform, going public at a ~$26B valuation. One of the landmark early-stage VC bets in European history.

### Depop
- **Outcome:** Acquired by Etsy for $1.625B, June 2021
- **Details:** Social commerce platform for second-hand fashion. Creandum backed the company early and supported it through significant growth in the US market before the Etsy acquisition.

### Trade Republic
- **Outcome:** Active — Unicorn ($5B+ valuation as of 2023)
- **Details:** European retail investing platform (stocks, ETFs, crypto). Now one of the leading fintech companies in Germany. Creandum was a seed investor.
