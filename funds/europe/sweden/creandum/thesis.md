# Creandum — Investment Thesis

## Thesis Summary

Creandum backs exceptional founders at seed stage before their potential is obvious to the market. Their core belief is that the best product wins — and that the best products come from founders with genuine conviction, customer obsession, and the willingness to take a decade-long journey. They commit fully to the founders they back, positioning themselves as the first call for both good news and bad news throughout the company's life.

## Stage Rationale

Seed-focused because Creandum believes the most transformative value-add comes in the earliest days — shaping product direction, building the team, and establishing the cultural foundation that determines whether a company becomes great. Their "Euro Seed 50" programme reflects a deliberate focus on this stage.

## Sector Rationale

Broad technology with a bias toward exceptional product experience. They don't chase sector trends — they follow founder quality. Historically strong in consumer (Spotify, Depop), fintech (Trade Republic, Taxfix), and more recently AI and climate.

## What They Look For

- Founders who have deep, genuine insight into the problem — not trend-chasers
- Exceptional product quality and design sensibility — "magical product experience"
- Global ambition from day one — they look for founders targeting large international markets
- A strong founding team with complementary skills; leadership as a competitive moat
- Data-driven iteration on top of strong initial intuition
- Long-term mindset — founders willing to spend a decade building something important

## What They Avoid

- Founders optimising for the exit over the mission
- Companies where network effects or distribution are the only moat (no product excellence)
- Short-term thinking or a founder unwilling to be challenged

Creandum reserves capital for follow-on and is a long-term partner through multiple rounds. They actively help portfolio companies connect with later-stage investors when the time comes.
