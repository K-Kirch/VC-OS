## TL;DR

Stockholm evergreen infrastructure investor founded 2020 by Tobias Emanuelsson and Karim Sahibzada. Not a traditional VC — runs an "infrastructure-as-a-service" model: co-founds and majority-owns new infrastructure companies (charging, energy storage, fiber/5G, rail/transport hubs), and also takes minority positions in deep-tech accelerating infrastructure (e.g., Einride). Relevant for VC mapping because they appear on Swedish growth-stage cap tables alongside venture capital — particularly green-transition deals.
