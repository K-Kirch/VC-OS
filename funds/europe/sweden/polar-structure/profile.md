## Description

Polar Structure is a Stockholm-based evergreen infrastructure investor founded in 2020 by Tobias Emanuelsson (also co-founder of Vault Investment Partners and Randviken Fastigheter) and Karim Sahibzada. The firm operates an "infrastructure-as-a-service" model: it co-founds and majority-owns new infrastructure companies in sustainable transport, green energy, and digital infrastructure — and also takes minority positions in deep-tech companies that accelerate infrastructure deployment (e.g., Einride). Its evergreen ownership structure means no fund-cycle pressure on hold periods. The firm's PSI Initiative Foundation receives donated annual profits.
