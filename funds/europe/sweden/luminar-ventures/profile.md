## Description

Luminar Ventures is the most active pre-seed and seed-stage VC fund in Sweden (self-described). Founded by serial entrepreneurs Jacob Key and Magnus Bergman. €125M AUM, 45+ portfolio companies, 2022 latest fund vintage. 90% of their pre-seed Fund I investments raised seed rounds. Focus on Nordic founders across B2B SaaS, open finance, climate, digital health, and consumer apps. [S — from website scrape 2026-05-10]
