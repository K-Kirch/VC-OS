# Luminar Ventures — Investment Thesis

## Thesis Summary

Luminar backs Nordic founders at pre-seed and seed who are relentlessly obsessed with solving a defined problem. As former founders and angel investors themselves, they emphasise hands-on support for finding product-market fit, enabling rapid growth, and laying the groundwork for international expansion. Their positioning: "the most active seed-stage VC fund in the Nordics."

## Stage Rationale

Pre-seed and seed exclusively. Luminar wants to be the first institutional money in, helping founders navigate the earliest and most uncertain phase — first hires, first products, first customers. 90% of their pre-seed Fund I investments subsequently raised seed rounds, which they cite as their proof of quality at this stage.

## Sector Rationale

Generalist by design across five defined buckets: Creative Consumer, Green Transition, Data Driven Enterprise, Digital Health, and Open Finance. They follow founder conviction and problem obsession, not sector heat.

## What They Look For

- **Relentlessness** — founders absolutely obsessed with solving the problem, inspiring their team the same way
- **Resourcefulness** — founders who find a way to get what they need, "hustle is the primary door-opener"
- **Transparency** — founders who never hide bad news, keep stakeholders informed of struggles as well as wins
- **Compassion** — founders who show empathy through turbulent times, prioritise people's wellbeing

## What They Avoid

- Founders optimising for the funding round rather than the problem
- Companies outside the Nordic geography (mandate is explicit)

## Fund Values

Luminar operates by four internal values they also look for in founders:
- **Honesty** — candid, real-time feedback; straight-talkers
- **Kindness** — empathy-first approach in every interaction
- **Ambition** — built the most active pre-seed/seed fund in Sweden; looking across the world for future technologies
- **Helpfulness** — helpful to every founder they meet, whether or not they invest
