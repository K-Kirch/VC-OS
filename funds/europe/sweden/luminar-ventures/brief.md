## TL;DR

Luminar is the most active pre-seed/seed fund in Sweden — 45 companies, €125M AUM, founded by serial entrepreneur-angels Jacob Key and Magnus Bergman. They back Nordic founders with a hands-on, operator-first approach. Small checks, deep involvement. 90% of pre-seed Fund I went on to raise seed rounds.

## Fit by Use Case

### Syndicating a deal
**Fit:** Medium
**Rationale:** Luminar leads at pre-seed/seed with small checks (€500K–€2.5M). Good syndication partner for deals requiring a lead with Nordic operator credibility. They will often be the first institutional check in a Nordic deal.
**Right contact:** Jacob Key or Magnus Bergman (GPs); Louise Hagen or Reid Jackson (Partners) for sector-specific deals

### Lining up the next round
**Fit:** Low–Medium
**Rationale:** Luminar is a pre-seed/seed fund — they cannot lead a Series A. Their value for next-round purposes is warm introductions to Nordic Series A funds and their track record of 90% of portfolio raising follow-on rounds.
**Right contact:** Jacob Key or Magnus Bergman for investor intros

### Competing against them
**They lead with:** Operator credibility (both GPs are former founders/angels), Nordic network depth, hands-on PMF support, speed at pre-seed
**Their weakness:** Small check size limits participation above seed; limited brand recognition outside Nordics
**How to differentiate:** Larger check, cross-border network, brand with international VCs for founders with global ambition from day one
