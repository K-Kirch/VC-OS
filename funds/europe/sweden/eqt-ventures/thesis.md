# EQT Ventures — Investment Thesis

## Thesis Summary

EQT Ventures backs founders building "Generation-Defining Companies" (GDCs) — companies that aim to shape the next decade, not just capture near-term market share. They combine early-stage conviction with the full resources of the EQT platform, including Motherbrain (their AI-powered deal sourcing tool) and access to EQT's global network of operators and portfolio companies. Their team of ex-founders and operators provides hands-on, "radical support" beyond capital.

## Stage Rationale

Seed through Series B because EQT Ventures believes the most important company-defining decisions happen early, and that having an experienced operator-investor at the table from the start compounds significantly over time. Their €2–50M range gives them flexibility to lead at multiple entry points.

## Sector Rationale

Sector-agnostic but with a strong lean toward technology businesses with network effects, scalability, and the potential for global reach. AI, enterprise software, and deep tech have been prominent in recent vintages, reflecting the EQT platform's technological orientation.

## What They Look For

- Founders building companies with potential to define or reshape an industry ("Generation-Defining")
- Big vision paired with pragmatic execution capability
- Teams that can scale — not just founding talent but the ability to attract and retain great people
- Companies that can use the EQT platform for strategic advantage (corporate partnerships, global expansion)
- Resilient business models that can withstand long development cycles

## What They Avoid

- Incremental improvements to existing markets without a step-change value proposition
- Founders who don't engage actively with the investment team post-close

## Operational Style

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| **Decision Speed** | Unknown |
| **Board Involvement** | Active — board seat standard; full EQT platform support |

## Follow-On Policy

EQT Ventures reserves capital for follow-on across the fund lifecycle. As part of EQT Group, portfolio companies can also access growth capital from EQT Growth and other EQT vehicles as they scale.
