## TL;DR

EQT Ventures is the early-stage arm of EQT Group (one of Europe's largest PE firms). They back "Generation-Defining Companies" with checks up to €50M and the full weight of the EQT platform behind them. Unique edge: Motherbrain, their AI-powered deal-sourcing engine. Team of ex-founders and operators.

## Fit by Use Case

### Syndicating a deal
**Fit:** High
**Rationale:** EQT Ventures leads rounds from seed to Series B and actively co-invests with European and global funds. Their platform team adds real operational leverage alongside the capital.
**Right contact:** Relevant partner by geography; check EQT Ventures team page

### Lining up the next round
**Fit:** High
**Rationale:** EQT Ventures has €2–50M range and can lead or participate at multiple stages. They also provide a bridge to EQT Growth for later-stage capital within the same ecosystem.
**Right contact:** Existing relationship partner within EQT Ventures team

### Competing against them
**They lead with:** EQT brand and global network, Motherbrain sourcing intelligence, operational platform, large check capacity
**Their weakness:** Larger fund = less personal than boutique seed funds; decision process may be slower
**How to differentiate:** Speed and conviction at seed; sector specialisation Motherbrain hasn't prioritised; relationships EQT can't replicate
