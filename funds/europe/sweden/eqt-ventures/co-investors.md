## Syndication Style

EQT Ventures typically leads rounds and brings in strategic co-investors who complement their EQT platform value-add. Given their large check capacity (€2–50M), they can lead rounds that smaller funds cannot. At growth stages, they can syndicate with EQT Growth and other large European and global funds.
