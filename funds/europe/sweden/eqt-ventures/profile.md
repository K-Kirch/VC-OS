## Description

EQT Ventures is the venture capital arm of EQT Group, one of Europe's largest alternative asset managers. Focused on backing "Generation-Defining Companies" from seed through growth, they invest €2–50M and bring the full EQT platform — including Motherbrain, their proprietary AI deal-sourcing tool — to bear on portfolio support. Team is composed of ex-founders and operators across six European and US offices.
