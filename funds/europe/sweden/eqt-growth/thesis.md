# EQT Growth — Investment Thesis

## Thesis Summary

Back strong management teams of growth-stage technology companies supported by secular macro trends, at the inflection point between product-market fit and scale. Apply EQT's full operational platform — Motherbrain AI, digitalization expertise, sustainability know-how, capital markets reach — to compress time-to-scale.

## Stage Rationale

Growth stage specifically because: (a) PMF de-risks the fundamental business; (b) the scaling phase is where EQT's operational expertise (built across €280B+ of parent-group activity) is most leverageable; (c) ticket size €50–250M sits between venture (too small) and large-cap PE buyout (different mandate).

## Sector Rationale

Five-sector mandate: Enterprise Tech, Consumer Tech, FinTech, Health Tech, Climate Tech. Reflects EQT's house view that secular macro tailwinds in digitalization, financial services modernization, healthcare technology, and energy transition will produce multiple multi-billion-euro growth winners over the fund vintage.

## What They Look For

- Growth-stage technology companies (post-PMF, in scaling phase)
- Strong management teams
- Exposure to secular macro tailwinds (digitalization, climate transition, etc.)
- European or US headquartered (Europe priority)
- Round size €50M+ accommodating €50–250M ticket
- Clear path to compounding scale

## What They Avoid

- Pre-PMF / venture-stage risk (handled by EQT Ventures)
- Buyout-stage / mature businesses (handled by EQT Private Equity)
- Sectors outside the five-sector mandate
