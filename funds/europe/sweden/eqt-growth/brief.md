## TL;DR

EQT Group's growth-stage technology vehicle, distinct from EQT Ventures. Stockholm-headquartered, €2.2B Fund I (2021) deploying €50–250M tickets into European and US growth-stage companies at the PMF-to-scale inflection. Backs strong management teams supported by secular macro trends; leverages EQT's full operational platform (Motherbrain AI, digitalization, sustainability, capital markets).

## Fit by Use Case

### Syndicating a deal
**Fit:** High for European growth-stage tech with clear secular tailwinds and ≥€50M round
**Rationale:** Active growth-stage lead with very large reserves; strong fit for European tech at scale-up.
**Right contact:** Dominik Stein (Head of Advisory) for European deals; Carolina Brochado for US/cross-border.

### Lining up the next round
**Fit:** High for portfolio companies approaching €50M+ rounds with €100M+ ARR trajectories or equivalent maturity
**Rationale:** Among the most active European growth funds; large check + EQT operational platform.
**Right contact:** Dominik Stein

### Competing against them
**They lead with:** EQT Group brand, Motherbrain AI sourcing, full-stack operational support, large ticket capacity
**Their weakness:** Process-heavy diligence at PE-fund scale; not a fast mover at the smaller end of growth
**How to differentiate:** Speed, founder-friendly terms, sector specialism
