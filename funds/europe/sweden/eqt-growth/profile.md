## Description

EQT Growth is the growth-stage technology vehicle of EQT Group, distinct from EQT Ventures (early-stage VC). Founded in 2021 with a €2.2B inaugural fund, the strategy invests €50–250M tickets into European and selectively-US growth-stage companies at the inflection point between product-market fit and scaling. Investments leverage EQT's full operational platform — Motherbrain (proprietary AI sourcing), digitalization, sustainability, and capital markets capabilities — alongside ~25 dedicated investment advisory professionals.
