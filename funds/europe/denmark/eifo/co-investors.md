# EIFO — Co-Investors & Network

## Lead / Follow Behavior

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| **Typically** | Follow |
| **Lead Round Frequency** | Never leads — co-investment mandate requires a private lead |

## Syndication Style

EIFO does not build syndicates — they join syndicates built by private VCs. The private lead investor is required before EIFO will engage. EIFO's presence in a round is a signal of Danish government backing but is not a substitute for a commercial lead.
