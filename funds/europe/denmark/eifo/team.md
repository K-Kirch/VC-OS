## Notes on Contacting EIFO

EIFO is a government institution — the process is more structured than a commercial VC. Initial contact is typically via their programme application process, not a cold reach to an individual. The relevant contact for a co-investment conversation is the Investment Director overseeing the relevant programme (venture, green transition, life science).
