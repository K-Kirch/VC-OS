## Description

EIFO (Eksport og Investeringsfonden) is the Danish state investment fund created by the merger of Vaekstfonden (the Danish Growth Fund) and EKF (Export Credit). On the venture side, EIFO provides co-investment capital alongside private VC funds into Danish startups and growth companies, operating as a market-correcting institution rather than a commercial investor.

> Note: EIFO acts as a co-investor, not a lead. Having EIFO in a round does not substitute for a private lead investor. Their presence can signal Danish government support but also comes with reporting requirements.
