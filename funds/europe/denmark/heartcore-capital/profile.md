## Description

Heartcore Capital is a European early-stage venture fund with Danish roots, backing consumer and SaaS founders from pre-seed through Series A. They focus on product-led companies with strong consumer or end-user engagement and have a reputation for being founder-friendly and hands-on at the earliest stages.
