# Heartcore Capital — Investment Thesis

## Thesis Summary

Heartcore Capital backs European consumer and SaaS founders at the earliest stages, with a particular emphasis on product quality and user experience. Their thesis is that Europe produces world-class product designers and engineers but has historically lacked early-stage capital from investors who understand consumer product dynamics. Heartcore positions itself as the partner of choice for founders building products that people love, not just tools they are forced to use.

## Stage Rationale

Pre-seed and seed because consumer and product-led SaaS companies need a VC partner who can help shape product strategy and go-to-market in the very early days — before growth mechanisms kick in. At later stages, growth-oriented investors can add more value than product-focused early VCs.

## Sector Rationale

Consumer and product-led SaaS because Heartcore believes the best European companies are built on product excellence — strong UX, clear value proposition, and organic growth loops. This is where European founders outperform US counterparts when given the right support.

## What They Look For

- Exceptional product quality — they look for products they would use themselves
- Founders who are obsessive about the user experience
- Consumer businesses with strong retention metrics or SaaS businesses with product-led growth signals
- Large European market opportunities with global extension potential
- Founders who can articulate a clear distribution strategy, not just a product vision

## What They Avoid

- Pure enterprise sales companies without a product-led component
- Companies where growth is entirely dependent on paid acquisition from day one
- Markets where product differentiation is not the primary competitive moat
