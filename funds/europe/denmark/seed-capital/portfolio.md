## Notable Exits & Unicorns

### Zendesk
- **Outcome:** IPO on NYSE (2014), subsequently acquired by private equity consortium for ~$10B (2022)
- **Details:** Seed Capital was an early Danish backer of Zendesk before its move to San Francisco. One of the most significant Danish VC exits in history.

### Vivino
- **Outcome:** Active — raised at $825M valuation; one of the world's largest wine marketplaces
- **Details:** Seed Capital backed Vivino from its early days as a Copenhagen startup. Company later raised international rounds from Elephant, Creandum and others.
