## TL;DR

One of Denmark's oldest and most credible seed funds, with landmark exits including Zendesk and Vivino. Deeply embedded in the Danish startup ecosystem with strong founder networks. The go-to Danish seed lead for Nordic B2B SaaS and consumer technology.

### Lining up the next round
**Fit:** High (for Seed → Series A transitions in Denmark/Nordics)

**How to differentiate:** Larger cheque or stronger international platform; sector specialisation if outside their core
