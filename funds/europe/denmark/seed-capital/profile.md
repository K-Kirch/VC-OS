## Description

Seed Capital is one of Denmark's oldest and most established early-stage venture funds, backing Nordic technology founders from the earliest stages. Known for deep operational involvement and long-term founder relationships, they have produced some of the most notable Danish tech exits including Zendesk and Vivino.
