# Seed Capital — Co-Investors & Network

## Lead / Follow Behavior

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| **Typically** | Lead |
| **Lead Round Frequency** | Leads the majority of initial investments |

## Syndication Style

Seed Capital typically leads rounds and builds syndicates of one or two complementary co-investors — often bringing in a specialist fund or an international seed fund alongside them. They prefer tight syndicates over large, complex cap tables at seed.
