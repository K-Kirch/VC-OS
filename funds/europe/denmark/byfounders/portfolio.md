## Notable Exits & Unicorns

### Pleo
- **Outcome:** Active unicorn — valued at $4.7B in 2021 Series C led by Bain Capital Ventures and Thrive Capital
- **Details:** byFounders led the seed round. Pleo is Denmark's most valuable VC-backed company and one of the strongest European fintech success stories.

### Granulate
- **Outcome:** Acquired by Intel in 2022 for approximately $650M
- **Details:** Israeli compute optimisation company backed at seed by byFounders; one of their first exits.

### Dune Analytics
- **Outcome:** Active — raised Series B at $1B valuation in 2022
- **Details:** Norwegian blockchain analytics platform; raised from a16z and others at growth stage.
