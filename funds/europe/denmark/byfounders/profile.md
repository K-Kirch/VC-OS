## Description

byFounders is a founder-community-driven venture fund backing pre-seed to Series A companies built by Nordic and Baltic founders. The fund is structured around a collective of experienced founders who actively contribute to portfolio companies alongside the investment team. Known for backing Pleo, Templafy, and Dixa.
