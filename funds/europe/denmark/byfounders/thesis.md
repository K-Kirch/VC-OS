# byFounders — Investment Thesis

## Thesis Summary

byFounders backs Nordic and Baltic founders building the next generation of global technology companies, typically from pre-seed through Series A. What differentiates them is their collective model: a community of 40+ experienced founders who co-invest time, introductions, and expertise alongside the fund's capital. Their thesis is that the best support for a founder comes from other founders who have navigated the same problems — not from career VCs alone.

## Stage Rationale

They focus on pre-seed to Series A because this is where founder-to-founder advice is most valuable — navigating product decisions, first hires, initial go-to-market, and the chaotic early years that career investors struggle to relate to experientially.

## Sector Rationale

Nordic and Baltic founders have consistently produced strong B2B SaaS, fintech, and developer tool companies due to the region's engineering depth, high digital literacy, and pragmatic commercial culture. byFounders leans into this rather than chasing sector trends.

## What They Look For

- Nordic or Baltic founders (born, educated, or built their first company in the region)
- Founders with a demonstrated obsession with the problem, not just the market opportunity
- Early signs of product-market fit — they prefer to invest in something working at small scale over a large pitch
- Teams that can sell — they care about commercial instinct as much as technical ability
- Companies with a clear path to €100M+ ARR, not lifestyle businesses
- Founders willing to engage genuinely with the collective, not just take the cheque

## What They Avoid

- Founders outside the Nordic/Baltic geography (their mandate is explicit)
- Companies without a credible European expansion story
- Founders looking for passive capital — the collective model requires engagement

## Operational Style

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| **Decision Speed** | Typically 2–6 weeks from first meeting to term sheet |
| **Board Involvement** | Active — board seat standard; collective founders available ad hoc |

## Follow-On Policy

byFounders reserves capital for follow-on and participates in Series A and B for top performers. They actively help portfolio companies raise follow-on by making introductions to their international VC network.
