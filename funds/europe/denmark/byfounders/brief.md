## TL;DR

Denmark's highest-profile early-stage fund, built around a collective of 40+ experienced founders. Backed Pleo (Denmark's only decacorn candidate), Templafy, and Dixa. Strong brand with Nordic/Baltic founders; good international co-investor relationships (Balderton, Index). The benchmark Danish seed fund for B2B founders.

## Fit by Use Case

### Syndicating a deal
**Fit:** High (if Pre-Seed to Series A, Nordic/Baltic B2B SaaS or fintech)
**Rationale:** Active lead with strong brand, decisive process, and a network that adds value beyond capital. The collective brings real founder expertise.
**Right contact:** Torben Majgaard or Asmus Friis Bie — verify sector coverage at byfounders.vc

### Lining up the next round
**Fit:** High (for Seed → Series A; strong intro network to Balderton, Index, Notion)
**Rationale:** byFounders explicitly positions helping raise the next round as a core service. Track record of connecting portfolio to top-tier Series A funds.
**Right contact:** Verify which GP covers the portfolio company's sector

### Competing against them
**They lead with:** Community/collective (differentiated), Pleo/brand halo, Nordic/Baltic focus
**Their weakness:** Mandate restricted to Nordic/Baltic founders — cannot invest outside the region; fund size limits participation at larger rounds
**How to differentiate:** If you have a stronger international platform or larger cheque; if the founder is outside Nordic/Baltic geography (byFounders cannot invest)
