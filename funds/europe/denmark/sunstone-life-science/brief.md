## TL;DR

One of the leading Nordic life science venture funds, with a track record of major pharma exits including Santaris Pharma (Roche, ~$450M) and Nuevolution (Amgen). Deep connections to Nordic university research pipelines and pharma industry. The first call for biotech and medtech in the Nordic region.
