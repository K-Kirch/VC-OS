## Description

Sunstone Life Science Ventures is one of the leading Nordic life science venture funds, specialising in biotech, medtech, and pharmaceutical companies. They back science-driven companies from early clinical stage through to late-stage development, primarily sourcing from the strong Nordic research base.

> Note: Sunstone Life Science Ventures is distinct from Sunstone Capital, which focuses on technology investments. Both are Danish-headquartered but operate as separate entities.
