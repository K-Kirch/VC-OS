# PreSeed Ventures — Co-Investors & Network

## Lead / Follow Behavior

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| **Typically** | Lead |
| **Lead Round Frequency** | Leads most pre-seed rounds; may co-lead at seed |

## Syndication Style

At pre-seed, PreSeed typically leads with a small cheque and brings in EIFO as a co-investor. They are active in helping portfolio companies raise their seed round from larger Danish and Nordic funds once milestones are hit.
