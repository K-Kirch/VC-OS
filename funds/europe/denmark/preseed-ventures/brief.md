## TL;DR

Denmark's earliest-stage specialist — one of the country's oldest pre-seed funds. Small tickets, high conviction, hands-on with Danish founders from day one. Often a first institutional investor that helps companies reach the stage where Seed Capital, byFounders, or international funds engage. Useful to know as a feeder fund for the Danish seed ecosystem.
