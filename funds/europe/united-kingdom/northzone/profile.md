## Description

Northzone is a pan-European and US venture fund with $3.1bn+ AUM and a 30-year track record. Founded in Oslo in 1996 and headquartered in London, they back founders from seed through growth with $1M–$50M checks. Known for backing Spotify, Klarna, and Trustpilot at early stages. Their platform includes a network of 500+ operators from leading companies including OpenAI, Anthropic, and Stripe.
