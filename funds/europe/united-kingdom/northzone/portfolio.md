## Notable Exits & Unicorns

### Spotify
- **Outcome:** NYSE IPO, April 2018
- **Details:** Northzone was one of the earliest institutional investors in Spotify, backing the company before it became the dominant global streaming platform. One of the best-returning VC bets in European history.

### Klarna
- **Outcome:** Active — Unicorn; IPO expected
- **Details:** Swedish BNPL and payments platform. Northzone backed Klarna early and has stayed on the cap table through significant growth. Valued at $6.7B as of last round (down from $45.6B peak in 2021).

### Tink
- **Outcome:** Acquired by Visa for €1.8B, March 2022
- **Details:** Open banking platform connecting banks and fintechs. Northzone was an early backer. The Visa acquisition was one of the largest European fintech exits.

### Trustpilot
- **Outcome:** IPO on Nasdaq Copenhagen, March 2021
- **Details:** Consumer review platform. Northzone backed Trustpilot through multiple growth rounds, culminating in the Copenhagen IPO.
