## TL;DR

Northzone is one of Europe's most storied VC firms — early backers of Spotify, Klarna, Trustpilot, and Kahoot. They invest from seed to growth with $1.2B latest fund, staying with founders from first check to IPO. London HQ with deep Nordic roots. Known for genuine founder empathy and 500+ operator network.

## Fit by Use Case

### Syndicating a deal
**Fit:** High
**Rationale:** Northzone leads from seed through growth and actively builds co-investor syndicates. Their brand attracts strong co-investors at every stage. They have the reserve capacity to double down and lead follow-on rounds.
**Right contact:** Partner matching the company's sector — Jeppe Zink (enterprise/SaaS), Paul Murphy (consumer/health), Wendy Xiao Schadeck (US-facing)

### Lining up the next round
**Fit:** Very High
**Rationale:** Northzone's fund size ($1.2B) and multi-stage mandate means they can lead or co-lead rounds from seed through growth without forcing a new lead. Multiple portfolio companies have gone from seed to IPO with Northzone on the cap table throughout.
**Right contact:** Existing relationship; Northzone partner already on board

### Competing against them
**They lead with:** Brand (Spotify/Klarna heritage), 500+ operator network, multi-stage follow-on capacity, genuine Nordic founder network
**Their weakness:** Large fund = cannot lead very small seed rounds (<$1M); may be slower than specialist micro-VCs at earliest stage
**How to differentiate:** Deeper sector specialisation; faster decision at pre-seed/seed; regional access Northzone doesn't have
