## Syndication Style

Northzone builds high-quality syndicates with a mix of European seed funds and global growth funds. At seed, they often co-invest with specialist Nordic funds. At Series A and beyond, they bring in US-based funds (Sequoia, Accel, General Catalyst) to give founders global optionality. They are known for maintaining long-term positions rather than flipping early.
