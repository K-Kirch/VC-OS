## Lead / Follow Behavior

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| **Typically** | Lead |
| **Lead Round Frequency** | Leads the vast majority of initial investments (first institutional check in 87% of portfolio) |

## Syndication Style

Inventure is almost always the first institutional investor. At seed, they often enter solo or with one angel/angel fund alongside them. They then actively help founders build the syndicate for Series A and beyond by making warm introductions to Nordic and international funds. They are not a growth-stage fund and do not compete with later-stage investors — they facilitate the handoff.
