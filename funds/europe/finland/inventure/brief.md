## TL;DR

Inventure is the leading Nordic/Baltic pre-seed and seed fund, founded in Helsinki in 2005. First institutional backer in 87% of their 100+ portfolio companies. Famous for Wolt (acquired by DoorDash for $8.1B). Backs a deliberately small number of founders per year with deep commitment — "ignite the few to accelerate the many."

## Fit by Use Case

### Syndicating a deal
**Fit:** Medium-High
**Rationale:** Inventure leads at pre-seed/seed and will co-invest with other Nordic and Baltic funds. They prefer tight syndicates with high-conviction co-investors. Best fit for deals with Nordic/Baltic founders.
**Right contact:** Helsinki-based partner — check Inventure team page for sector alignment

### Lining up the next round
**Fit:** Medium
**Rationale:** Inventure is a seed fund — they reserve capital for follow-on but cannot lead Series A+ rounds themselves. Their real value is warm introductions to larger Nordic funds (Northzone, EQT Ventures) and international funds familiar with Nordic ecosystems.
**Right contact:** Inventure partner already on the board

### Competing against them
**They lead with:** First-mover conviction, Nordic/Baltic network depth, founder reputation (Wolt), willingness to back under uncertainty
**Their weakness:** Small check size relative to later-stage needs; geographic mandate limits them to Nordics/Baltics
**How to differentiate:** Larger check if above €5M; non-Nordic geography if founder is elsewhere
