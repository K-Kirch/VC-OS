## Description

Inventure is a Nordic and Baltic early-stage generalist fund, founded in Helsinki in 2005. They describe their mandate as "igniting the few to accelerate the many" — backing a small number of ambitious founding teams per year with high conviction. They have been the first institutional investor in 87% of their 100+ portfolio companies, and have backed Wolt, Swappie, Algorithmiq, and ReOrbit.
