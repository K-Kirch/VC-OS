## Notable Exits & Unicorns

### Wolt
- **Outcome:** Acquired by DoorDash for $8.1B, May 2022
- **Details:** Inventure was the first institutional investor in Wolt, backing the company at seed when it was a pivot from a restaurant booking app. Wolt grew into a dominant food and grocery delivery platform across Europe and the Middle East before the DoorDash acquisition. The defining portfolio company for Inventure's brand.

### Aiven
- **Outcome:** Active — Unicorn ($3B valuation, 2022)
- **Details:** Cloud database/infrastructure platform (managed open-source services: PostgreSQL, Kafka, OpenSearch). Raised $210M Series D at $3B valuation. One of Finland's most successful B2B SaaS companies.

### Swappie
- **Outcome:** Active — Profitable circular economy marketplace
- **Details:** Refurbished iPhone marketplace. Pivoted from a different business model. Now profitable and one of the leading recommerce platforms in Europe. Cited by Inventure as a proof point of their pivot-tolerance thesis.
